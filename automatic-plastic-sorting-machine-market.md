<p><strong>The Automatic Plastic Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Sorting Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Sorting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Plastic Sorting Machine market primarily features two types: Chute-Type Sorting Machines and Belt-Type Sorting Machines. Chute-Type Sorting Machines utilize gravity to guide plastic materials through a funnel system for separation based on density and size, making them efficient for bulk sorting. In contrast, Belt-Type Sorting Machines transport plastics along conveyor belts equipped with sensors or cameras, allowing for precise sorting by material type or color. Both types enhance recycling processes and improve operational efficiency in waste management.</p></p>
