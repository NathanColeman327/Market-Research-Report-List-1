<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems Market can be categorized into two primary types: Pneumatic EGR Valves and Electric EGR Valves. Pneumatic EGR Valves utilize vacuum pressure generated by the engine to control recirculation, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, Electric EGR Valves employ electric actuators for precise control, enhancing efficiency and adaptability in various driving conditions. Both types aim to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions, improve overall engine performance, and meet stringent environmental regulations in modern vehicles.</p></p>
