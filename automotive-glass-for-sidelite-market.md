<p><strong>The Automotive Glass for Sidelite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive glass sidelites market comprises three primary types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength and safety, shattering into small, blunt pieces to reduce injury risk. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers with an interlayer for enhanced safety and sound insulation, resisting shattering upon impact. The "others" category includes specialized glass types, such as acoustic or tinted options, catering to specific design or functional requirements in automotive applications. Together, these types enhance vehicle safety, performance, and aesthetics.</p></p>
