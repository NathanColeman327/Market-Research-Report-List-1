<p><p>C C Composite market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, with Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation processes being prominent. CVD involves depositing thin films of materials onto substrates, enhancing properties like hardness and thermal resistance, crucial for aerospace and electronics. Meanwhile, Liquid Impregnation entails soaking a porous substrate with a liquid resin to improve strength and durability, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. Both methods significantly contribute to advancements in composite materials, tailoring performance for specific applications.</p></p>
