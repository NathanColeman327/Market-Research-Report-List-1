<p><p>The circulatory support devices market encompasses technologies that assist heart function. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) aid the heart by pumping blood, typically used in patients with severe heart failure. Total Artificial Hearts (TAHs) replace the entire heart, serving as a temporary or permanent solution for those with end-stage heart failure. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) provides life support by oxygenating blood outside the body, often used in critical conditions like cardiac arrest or severe respiratory failure, facilitating recovery while supporting circulation.</p></p>
