<p><p>Cocoa roasters are categorized into several market types based on their design and operational methods. Continuous roasters facilitate uninterrupted processing, ideal for high-volume production. Nib drum roasters employ rotating drums to evenly roast cocoa nibs, enhancing flavor. Compact cylindrical roasters are designed for smaller spaces, offering efficient roasting for artisanal producers. Cylindrical batch roasters allow for versatile, batch-wise roasting, catering to varying production needs. Other roaster types may include specialized designs for unique cocoa processing techniques, expanding market diversity.</p></p>
