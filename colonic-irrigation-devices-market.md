<p><ul><li>Open System Machine</li><li>Close System Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colonic irrigation devices market is primarily categorized into open system machines and closed system machines. Open system machines use gravity for water flow, allowing patients to control the process and typically requiring a healthcare professional for setup. In contrast, closed system machines are more automated, providing a sealed environment that minimizes contamination risk, facilitating a safer and more hygienic procedure. Both systems cater to different patient preferences and clinic requirements, driving their respective market segments.</p></p>
