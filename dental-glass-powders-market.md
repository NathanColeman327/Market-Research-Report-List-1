<p><strong>The Dental Glass Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Glass Powders</li><li>Reactive Glass Powders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental glass powders market is categorized into two main types: inert glass powders and reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders are chemically stable and used primarily as fillers in dental applications, enhancing the properties of dental materials without participating in chemical reactions. In contrast, reactive glass powders engage in chemical interactions, contributing to processes like bonding and setting in dental cements and restorative materials. Both types are essential for achieving optimal performance in dental treatments and products.</p></p>
