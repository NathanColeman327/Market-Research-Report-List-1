<p><p>The dewatering pumps market is primarily divided into submersible and non-submersible types. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to operate underwater, efficiently removing water from confined spaces like construction sites and mines. They are compact and portable, making them ideal for tight areas. Conversely, non-submersible dewatering pumps, often mounted above ground, utilize suction to draw water from lower levels. These are commonly used in municipal applications and larger-scale projects where pumping needs are significant and mobility is essential.</p></p>
