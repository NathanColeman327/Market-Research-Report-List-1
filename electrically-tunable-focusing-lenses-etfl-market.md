<p><p>Electrically Tunable-Focusing Lenses (ETFL) are innovative optical devices that adjust their focal lengths using electrical signals, enabling dynamic focus changes. The market is categorized into Flat Convex and Flat Concave lenses. Flat Convex lenses converge light rays, ideal for applications requiring magnification or detail, while Flat Concave lenses diverge light, suited for applications needing beam expansion or wider field views. Both types enhance flexibility in imaging systems, telecommunication, and projection technologies, driving advancements in optical solutions.</p></p>
