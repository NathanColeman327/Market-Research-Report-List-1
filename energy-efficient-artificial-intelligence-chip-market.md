<p><p>The energy-efficient artificial intelligence chip market comprises several types: GPUs, ASICs, FPGAs, and neuromorphic chips. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) provide flexibility, allowing customization for varying workloads. Neuromorphic chips mimic human brain functions and are designed for low-power, high-speed processing. Each type serves distinct needs in AI applications, balancing performance and energy consumption.</p></p>
