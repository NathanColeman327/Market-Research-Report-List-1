<p><ul><li>Polarization Dependent Isolator</li><li>Polarization Independent Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Free Space Isolators market is divided into two main types: Polarization Dependent Isolators (PDI) and Polarization Independent Isolators (PII). PDI devices exploit specific light polarization states for optimal performance, making them ideal in applications where polarization can be controlled. In contrast, PII devices are designed to function effectively regardless of the polarization state, offering versatility in various environments. Both types are crucial in minimizing feedback in laser systems, enhancing signal stability in optical communications.</p></p>
