<p><p>GaN power modules are categorized into two main market types: Embedded Type and Ordinary Type. Embedded Type modules integrate Gallium Nitride devices directly into circuit boards, offering compact designs and improved thermal management for high-efficiency applications. In contrast, Ordinary Type modules are standalone units that encapsulate GaN technology, allowing for easier scalability and replacement in existing systems. Both types enhance power density and efficiency, making them essential for applications like electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and advanced computing.</p></p>
