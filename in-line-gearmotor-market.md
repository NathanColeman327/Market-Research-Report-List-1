<p><p>In the inline gearmotor market, three main types dominate: planetary, helical, and helical bevel. Planetary gearmotors offer high torque and compactness, making them ideal for precision applications. Helical gearmotors provide smooth operation and high efficiency, suitable for various industrial uses due to their ability to handle heavy loads. Helical bevel gearmotors combine helical gearing with bevel gear arrangements, allowing for right-angle drive configurations, which enhances versatility in space-constrained environments while maintaining efficiency and performance.</p></p>
