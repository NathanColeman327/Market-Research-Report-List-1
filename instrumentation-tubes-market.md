<p><p>The instrumentation tubes market is primarily categorized into seamless and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured from solid metal billets and have no joints, offering enhanced strength and reliability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welded tubes are formed by welding metal strips or plates, providing cost-effectiveness and versatility for various applications. Both types are essential in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and manufacturing, tailored to specific requirements based on pressure, temperature, and fluid compatibility.</p></p>
