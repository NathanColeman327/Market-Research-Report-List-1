<p><ul><li>Intramuscular Injection</li><li>Subcutaneous Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IVF syringes market is primarily categorized into two types based on the injection method: intramuscular and subcutaneous. Intramuscular injections deliver medication deep into the muscle, allowing for rapid absorption, often used for hormones like hCG. Subcutaneous injections, on the other hand, administer drugs into the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, providing a slower and steadier release, ideal for medications like FSH. Both methods are crucial for various steps in the IVF process, optimizing treatment efficacy.</p></p>
