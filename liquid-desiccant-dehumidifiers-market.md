<p><p>The Liquid Desiccant Dehumidifiers market is categorized into two main types based on the solvent used: organic solvents and inorganic solvents. Organic solvent-based dehumidifiers utilize substances like glycols and esters, which effectively absorb moisture while being less corrosive. In contrast, inorganic solvent-based dehumidifiers use compounds like lithium chloride or calcium chloride, which are highly effective in moisture absorption but can be more corrosive. Each type caters to specific applications, balancing efficiency, cost, and environmental impacts.</p></p>
