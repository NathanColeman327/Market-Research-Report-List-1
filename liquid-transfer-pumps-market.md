<p><p>The liquid transfer pumps market encompasses various types, including AC transfer pumps, DC transfer pumps, and hand transfer pumps. AC transfer pumps are powered by alternating current, suitable for high-capacity applications in industrial settings. DC transfer pumps operate on direct current, making them ideal for portable use and applications where electricity is not readily available. Hand transfer pumps are manually operated, providing a simple and efficient solution for small-scale fluid transfer tasks without the need for power sources.</p></p>
