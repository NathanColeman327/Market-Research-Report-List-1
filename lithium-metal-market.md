<p><ul><li>Salt Lake Brine</li><li>Lithium Ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium metal market comprises primarily two types: salt lake brine and lithium ore. Salt lake brine involves extracting lithium from subterranean brine pools through evaporation, which is typically more cost-effective and has a lower environmental impact. In contrast, lithium ore extraction involves mining hard rock deposits, such as spodumene, and is generally more energy-intensive and expensive. Both methods supply lithium, essential for battery production, and cater to the growing demand driven by electric vehicles and renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
