<p><p>The marine fire damper market is segmented into two primary actuator types: electrical and manual actuators. Electrical actuators utilize electrical signals for automatic operation, providing faster responsiveness and integration with fire safety systems. In contrast, manual actuators require physical operation to close or open the damper, offering simplicity and reliability in environments with limited electrical access. Both types are crucial for ensuring effective smoke containment and fire prevention in marine vessels, enhancing safety and compliance with maritime regulations.</p></p>
