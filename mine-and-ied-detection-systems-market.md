<p><p>The mine and IED detection systems market comprises three main types: sensor-based, radar-based, and laser-based systems. Sensor-based systems utilize ground-penetrating radar and electronic sensors to detect explosive materials and landmines. Radar-based systems employ microwave radar technology to identify buried threats through abnormal reflections. Laser-based detection leverages laser beams to identify and analyze disturbances in the surface, often enhancing detection accuracy. Each type offers distinct advantages in real-time detection and security, contributing to safer environments in conflict zones and high-risk areas.</p></p>
