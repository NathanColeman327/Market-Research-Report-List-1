<p><ul><li>Freezing Point Osmometer</li><li>Vapro Osmometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The osmometer market is primarily segmented into three types: freezing point osmometers, vapor osmometers, and others. Freezing point osmometers measure the osmotic concentration by determining the freezing point depression of a solution, making them ideal for clinical settings. Vapor osmometers, on the other hand, assess osmotic pressure by measuring the vapor pressure of a solvent, often used in research and industrial applications. The "others" category includes specialized devices designed for niche applications or unique measurement techniques within the osmometry field.</p></p>
