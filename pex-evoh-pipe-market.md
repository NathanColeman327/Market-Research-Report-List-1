<p><p>The PEX-EVOH pipe market encompasses several types, primarily differentiated by their manufacturing processes and compositions. PEX-A pipes are characterized by their superior flexibility and resistance to cracking. PEX-B pipes are known for their affordability and easier installation, often produced through a continuous process. PEX-C pipes offer enhanced dimensional stability and are typically cross-linked via irradiation. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine PEX with an aluminum layer, providing excellent barrier properties and pressure resistance, making them suitable for diverse plumbing and heating applications.</p></p>
