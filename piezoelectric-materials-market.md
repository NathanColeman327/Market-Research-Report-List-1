<p><p>The piezoelectric materials market encompasses several types: ceramics, polymers, composites, and others. Ceramics, like lead zirconate titanate (PZT), offer high sensitivity and stability, making them widely used in sensors and actuators. Polymers, such as polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), are flexible and lightweight, suitable for various applications. Composites combine the properties of ceramics and polymers to enhance performance and durability. Other materials may include natural crystals or new innovative substances, expanding options for diverse technological applications.</p></p>
