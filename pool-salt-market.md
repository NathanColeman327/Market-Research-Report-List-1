<p><strong>The Pool Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool salt market comprises three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and typically contains impurities, making it less refined. Solar salt is derived from evaporating seawater or salt brine in large ponds, resulting in a more natural and pure product. Evaporated salt is produced through heating brine to remove water, leading to high purity levels. Each type serves specific applications in pool maintenance, influencing water quality and equipment longevity.</p></p>
