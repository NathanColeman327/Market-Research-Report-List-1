<p><p>The portable moisture analyzers market consists of various types that cater to diverse moisture measurement needs. Microwave analyzers utilize microwave radiation to quickly determine moisture content by measuring the dielectric properties of materials. Infrared analyzers employ infrared light absorption to assess moisture levels, offering rapid results. Loss on Drying (LOD) methods involve weighing samples before and after drying to calculate moisture loss. Other methods may include capacitance and resistance-based technologies, providing additional options for accurate moisture analysis in portable formats.</p></p>
