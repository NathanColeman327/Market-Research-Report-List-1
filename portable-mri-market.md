<p><p>The Portable MRI market is segmented into three main types: Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI, which offers high-resolution imaging and requires cryogenic cooling; Electromagnet-based Portable MRI, utilizing electromagnetic coils for imaging while being less bulky than superconducting models; and Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI, which employs permanent magnets for a compact design, making it easier to transport. Each type caters to different clinical needs, balancing image quality, portability, and operational costs in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
