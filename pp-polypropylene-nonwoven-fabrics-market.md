<p><p>The PP (Polypropylene) nonwoven fabrics market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Spunbonded** - Produced by bonding continuous filaments, this type is durable and commonly used in products like masks and geotextiles. </p><p>2. **Staples** - Made from short fiber lengths, staple nonwovens are soft and absorbent, often used in hygiene products. </p><p>3. **Composite** - These combine various nonwoven types for enhanced properties, targeting specialized applications. </p><p>4. **Meltblown** - Created using high-velocity air, these fabrics are fine and ideal for filtration and medical applications.</p></p>
