<p><p>Product LifeCycle Management (PLM) focuses on managing a product's entire lifecycle, from inception and design to production and retirement. On-Premise solutions require local installation and maintenance, offering greater control but often higher upfront costs. Both approaches aim to enhance efficiency and streamline product development processes.</p></p>
