<p><p>Quality Management Software (QMS) is categorized into three main types: Cloud-Based, Web-Based, and On-Premise. Cloud-Based QMS allows users to access the software remotely via the internet, offering scalability and lower upfront costs. Web-Based QMS operates through a web browser, typically requiring no installation, facilitating easy access and updates. On-Premise QMS is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control and security but requiring significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs.</p></p>
