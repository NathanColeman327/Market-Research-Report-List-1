<p><p>The radiopharmaceutical synthesizers market encompasses three main types: cyclotrons, nuclear reactors, and radionuclide generators. Cyclotrons are compact particle accelerators that produce short-lived isotopes for medical imaging and therapy. Nuclear reactors generate a wide range of isotopes through nuclear fission, primarily for diagnostic and therapeutic applications. Radionuclide generators are systems that extract specific isotopes, such as technetium-99m, from a parent nuclide, offering a convenient and cost-effective method for generating radiopharmaceuticals. Together, these technologies support advancements in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
