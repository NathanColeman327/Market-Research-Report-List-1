<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor IC photomask market primarily consists of two types: quartz masks and soda-lime glass masks. Quartz masks, made from high-purity quartz, are preferred for their superior thermal and optical properties, enabling high-resolution patterning essential for advanced semiconductor fabrication. On the other hand, soda-lime glass masks are more cost-effective and suitable for less critical applications, though they offer lower durability and precision compared to quartz. The choice between these types depends on specific manufacturing requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
