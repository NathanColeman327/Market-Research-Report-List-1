<p><ul><li>Peroxide Cure Pressure Sensitive Adhesives</li><li>Platinum Cure Pressure Sensitive Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone-based pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) are categorized into two main types: peroxide cure and platinum cure adhesives. Peroxide cure PSAs rely on thermal curing, where heat activates peroxide compounds to cross-link silicone polymers, yielding durable adhesive properties. In contrast, platinum cure PSAs utilize platinum catalysts for curing at lower temperatures, ensuring enhanced clarity, flexibility, and adhesion qualities. Both types offer unique benefits, such as high-temperature resistance and excellent bonding to various substrates, making them suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
