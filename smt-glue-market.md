<p><ul><li>Epoxy SMT Glue</li><li>Polyene Organic SMT Glue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMT Glue markets are primarily categorized into Epoxy SMT Glue and Polyene Organic SMT Glue. Epoxy SMT Glue is a thermosetting adhesive known for its excellent bonding strength, thermal resistance, and durability, making it ideal for electronic applications. In contrast, Polyene Organic SMT Glue offers flexibility and quick curing times, suitable for high-speed production processes. Both types are essential in surface mount technology, facilitating the attachment of electronic components to circuit boards, thus enhancing functionality and performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
