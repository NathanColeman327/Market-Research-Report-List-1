<p><p>The spunmelt nonwoven fabrics market primarily consists of two types: PET (polyethylene terephthalate) and PP (polypropylene). In contrast, PP nonwovens are lightweight, cost-effective, and widely used in various sectors, including agriculture and filtration. Both materials are favored for their versatility and ability to be engineered for specific properties, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
