<p><p>The sugar dissolving machines market is segmented into two main types: batch dissolution and continuous dissolution. Batch dissolution involves processing a specific quantity of sugar in a single cycle, ideal for small-scale operations and precise formulation. In contrast, continuous dissolution systems operate non-stop, allowing for consistent sugar integration into liquids, making them suitable for large-scale production in industries like beverages. Both types cater to varied production needs, ensuring efficiency and optimizing sugar incorporation in food and drink processes.</p></p>
