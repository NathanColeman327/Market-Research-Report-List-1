<p><ul><li>Buck Regulators (Step-Down)</li><li>Boost Regulators (Step-Up)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switching regulators market includes buck (step-down) and boost (step-up) regulators, each serving distinct applications in power management. Buck regulators efficiently lower voltage from a higher input, making them ideal for devices requiring lower power levels. Conversely, boost regulators increase voltage from a lower input, essential for applications that need higher power. Both types are crucial in various sectors such as consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial systems, driven by the demand for energy efficiency and compact designs in electronic devices.</p></p>
