<p><p>The Tabletop SEM market includes various scanning electron microscope types, primarily W-SEM, FEG-SEM, and FIB-SEM. W-SEM, or tungsten-based SEM, offers a balance of affordability and imaging quality, suitable for general applications. FEG-SEM, or field emission gun SEM, provides higher resolution and superior imaging for advanced research. FIB-SEM, or focused ion beam SEM, combines imaging and milling capabilities, enabling precise material modification and analysis. These systems are widely used in industries like materials science, electronics, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
