<p><p>The X-ray Diffraction Equipment market primarily comprises two types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is used to analyze crystalline materials in powdered form, enabling the determination of phase identification, crystal structure, and other properties. In contrast, Single-crystal XRD focuses on analyzing individual crystals, providing detailed information about the molecular and atomic structure. Both methods are essential for materials characterization in various fields such as chemistry, biology, and materials science, driving advancements in research and industrial applications.</p></p>
